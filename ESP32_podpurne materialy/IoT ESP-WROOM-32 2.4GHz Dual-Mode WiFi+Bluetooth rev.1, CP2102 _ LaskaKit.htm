    Product detailed description

    The ESP-WROOM-32 is a powerful module that targets a wide range of applications, from low-power sensor networks to the most demanding tasks such as voice encoding, music streaming and MP3 decoding or web-enabled IoT communication devices.

    The development board is based on a sufficiently powerful dual-core Tensilica LX6 processor which can be individually controlled, their clock frequency is adjustable from 80 MHz to 240 MHz, 520kB of RAM is included, the internal 4MB SPI Flash memory is also large enough for more complex applications. The module can communicate wirelessly over WiFi 2.4GHz and Bluetooth 4.0 thanks to the integrated antenna. USB interface of the CP2102 controller from which the module can be powered and programmed.

    ESP32 integrates a rich set of peripherals, capacitive touch sensors, Hall sensors, low noise sensing amplifiers, high speed SDIO interface up to 50MHz, SD/MMC cards and Flash memory expansion via SPI up to 16MB, 3x UART, 3x SPI, 2x I2S, 2x I2C.
    There are 26 digital I/O pins, 16 PMW pins, 18 analog inputs, 2 digital-to-analog DACs, 8 channels for infrared control.

    The board is compatible with Arduino IDE and NodeMCU.

    Specifications:

    • SoC: ESP32-D0WDQ6 (revision 1)
    • CPU frequency 80 to 240 MHz
    • Program flash 4MB
    • SRAM 520 KB
    • WiFi 2.4-2.5GHz 802.11bgn (802.11n up to 150 Mbps)
      • wifi mode - Station/softAP/SoftAP+station/P2P
      • wPA/WPA2/WPA2-Enterprise/WPS security
    • Bluetooth v4.2 BR/EDR and BLE, CVSD and SBC audio support
    • Hardware Accelerated Encryption AES/SHA2/Elliptical Curve Cryptography/RSA-4096
    • SD/SDIO/CE-ATA/MMC/eMMC memory module interfaces
    • Communication interfaces 3xUART, 3xSPI, SDIO, 2xI2C, 2xI2S, IR
    • Input/output interface 34xGPIO, 18xADC, 2xDAC, 16xLED PWM, LNA, capacitive touch sensors, hall sensors
    • RTC with separate 32kHz oscillator
    • Internal 26MHz oscillator for CPU
    • Various low power modes up to 3mA in active mode, 2.5uA in hibernation.
    • Integrated LED: GPIO1
    • Board dimensions: approx 55x28mm

    Included in delivery:

    • 1pc IoT ESP-WROOM-32 2.4GHz Dual-Mode WiFi+Bluetooth rev.1, CP2102

    Notice:

    • There is one pin marked wrong on the board! The name of the GND signal is incorrectly listed under the 5V pin on the board. It should be CMD correctly. So check your wiring according to the pinout picture available in our photo gallery.

    Remark:

    • This is a 3.3V device. If you plug it into 5V digital sensors or devices, you may need a low-level converter like our 4-channel or 8-channel converter modules.
    • This product is not a standalone functional unit and may require professional installation.
    • Product images are for illustration purposes only and may sometimes differ from the actual appearance of the item. However, this does not change its basic properties.
